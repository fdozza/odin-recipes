French Toast

Description

A warm, delicious, sweet and savory dish that can be enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, and dinner!

Ingredients

  • Day old bread
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 Tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1 Tsp. ground cinammon
  • 1 Tbsp. powdered sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. grapeseed oil
  • Syrup of choice

Steps

  1. Crack open eggs in a bowl and whisk.
  2. Pour in whole milk into egg mixture, use the vanilla extract and cinammon, and whisk.
  3. Heat a pan or a skillet on medium heat and prep the bread.
  4. Grab the bread and submerge in the egg mixture, ensuring both sides of the bread of soaked.
  5. Drop the egg soaked bread in the pan and cook for 3 1/2 to 4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Repeat for the remaining bread piece.
  6. Garnish the french toast with the powdered sugar and finish off with your syrup of choice.
